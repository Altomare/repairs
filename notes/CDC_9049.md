Specs:

- 48 TPI
- 40 tracks per side
- 2 sided

## Jumpers

Jumpers description:
- H: Motor on & head load with select (HS)
- 1: Drive select 1
- 2: Drive select 2
- 3: Drive select 3
- X: Multiplex opt
- 4: Drive select 4
- M: Head load with 'motor on' signal only
